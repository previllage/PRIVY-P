About Privy Pink

Privy Pink is a small-batch, home-based business creating natural hair and body products. We use carefully selected ingredients — olive oil, coconut oil, vitamin E, rosemary and chebe — to make products that nourish hair and skin. Our mission is to provide affordable, effective and lovingly-made products.
